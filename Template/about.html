KalaKart is an ecommerce platform created to support local artists and promote handmade products. Our goal is to provide a simple and reliable marketplace where creators can showcase their work and customers can discover unique handmade and cultural products.

Our mission is to help artists reach a wider audience by providing an easy and reliable online platform. We aim to make handmade products more accessible while encouraging creativity and supporting local talent.
